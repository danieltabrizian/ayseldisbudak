# Aysel Disbudak - Brand Book Technical Summary

## 1. Logo (Pages 04-07)
- **Primary Mark:** "AD" Monogram with "AYSEL DISBUDAK" text below.
- **Clear Space:** Minimum clear space defined by the x-height of the letter "D" around the logo.
- **Variations:**
    - **Full Color:** Dark Purple monogram on Light Blue background.
    - **Inverted:** White monogram on Dark Purple background.
    - **Monochrome:** Solid Black or White for restricted color applications.
- **Misuse:**
    - Do not rotate or distort.
    - Do not add drop shadows or effects.
    - Do not place on busy backgrounds without a solid container.

## 2. Colors (Updated)
### Primary Palette
- **Aysel Purple (Primair):** `#2e1a34`
    - Usage: Main brand color. Headers, banners, backgrounds. Always use for text on Cool White backgrounds.
- **Aysel Red (Secundair):** `#621b1f`
    - Usage: Accents, CTAs (Buttons), icons, hover effects. Use sparingly for energy.

### Secondary & Alternative
- **Bright Purple (Alternatief):** `#320033`
    - Usage: Alternative background for sections or quotes to create variation within the purple spectrum.
- **Cool White (Lichte tint):** `#e3ebf0`
    - Usage: Subtle background for text areas or full pages. Creates a fresh, clean look.
- **Light Blue (Lichte tint):** `#dde5ee`
    - Usage: Supportive background for sections or text color on dark backgrounds (Purple/Red).

## 3. Typography (Pages 17-19)
### Headings (Titels)
- **Font:** `Italiana`
- **Weight:** Regular (400)
- **Style:** Uppercase or Sentence Case.
- **Letter Spacing:** Wide tracking (e.g., `0.02em` to `0.05em`).
- **Usage:** `h1`, `h2`, `h3`.

### Body Text (Body)
- **Font:** `Raleway`
- **Weights:**
    - **Extra Light (200):** Large intro text, quotes.
    - **Light (300):** Standard body copy.
    - **Regular (400):** Buttons, navigation, emphasis.
- **Usage:** Paragraphs, UI elements, metadata.

## 4. Design Elements (Pages 22-23)
### Thin Lines (Dunne Lijnen)
- **Style:** 1px solid line.
- **Color:** Aysel Paars (on light) or White (on dark).
- **Usage:** Separating sections, underlining headers, defining grid areas.

### Gradients (Gradient Achtergrond)
- **Style:** Subtle, linear gradients.
- **Direction:** Top-left to bottom-right (approx 135deg).
- **Variations:**
    - Deep Purple -> Slightly lighter Purple.
    - Pale Blue -> White.

## 5. Photography (Page 24)
- **Style:** Authentic, warm, professional.
- **Subjects:** Portraits of Aysel, interaction with clients, abstract textures (nature, books).
- **Treatment:** Natural lighting, consistent color grading to match the brand palette (warm tones, soft contrast).

## 6. Web Implementation Guidelines
- **Header:** Sticky, glassmorphism effect. Logo inverted (White) on Dark Purple angled corner.
- **Layout:** Generous whitespace (padding/margin). Asymmetric grids.
- **Buttons:** Ghost buttons (transparent with border) or solid Deep Purple. Uppercase text with wide tracking.
- **Interaction:** Subtle hover effects (lines expanding, opacity changes).
